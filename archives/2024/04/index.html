About me

I am a senior-year undergraduate student at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), under the supervision of ‪Shiguang Shan‬ and ‪Dongbin Zhao. Currently, I am working as a research intern at the Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), collaborating with Dr. Zike Yan. In the fall semester of 2024, I will begin my Master’s studies in Robotics at the Georgia Institute of Technology within the College of Computing.

My research interests are centered around robotics and computer vision. I am dedicated to develop more human-like robot systems, with the capacity to adapt to unseen scenarios and the ability to integrate multi-modal information, such as language instructions and semantic cues, to enable more efficient task planning and execution.

I am an enthusiast for dancing and occasionally engage in choreography. I enjoy the moment of letting my body flow freely, often stumbling upon exciting new movements. I am also quite into basketball — admittedly more of an enthusiastic trier than a skilled player — and I thoroughly enjoy hiking.
